About Star Therapy

Rooted in Community, Driven by Care

Star Therapy, LLC was founded to fill a critical gap in El Paso’s healthcare landscape. Our mission is to provide high-quality, culturally responsive speech-language pathology services to children and adults in El Paso’s underserved communities.

With 15 years of clinical experience in school and clinical settings, and deep roots in border community culture, we understand that effective therapy means meeting families where they are—in their neighborhoods, in their homes, and in their language.
