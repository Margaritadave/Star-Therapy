About Star Therapy

Rooted in Community, Driven by Care

Star Bilingual Speech Language Therapy was founded to fill a critical gap in El Paso’s healthcare landscape. Our mission is to provide high-quality, culturally responsive speech-language pathology services to children and adults in El Paso’s underserved communities.

With years of clinical experience in school and clinical settings, and deep roots in border community culture, we understand that effective therapy means meeting families where they are—in their neighborhoods, in their homes, and in their language.

Comprehensive SLP Care Across the Lifespan

From a toddler’s first words to an elder’s swallowing safety—we provide expert care at every stage.

Pediatric Speech & Language

Voice & Swallowing

Treatment for voice disorders, vocal strain, dysphagia, and post-surgical rehabilitation for adults and geriatric patients.

All Ages

Cognitive Communication

Therapy for post-stroke aphasia, TBI recovery, dementia-related communication decline, and neurodegenerative conditions.

Adult & Geriatric

AAC Services

Augmentative and alternative communication evaluations, device trials, training, and ongoing support for nonverbal or minimally verbal clients.

All Ages

Bilingual Evaluations
